# Quality Checklist

## Text

Check all visible text before finalizing:

- English-only by default.
- No accidental Chinese text unless requested.
- No accidental mixed-language labels by default.
- Correct technical acronyms: AGI, ASI, UAI, AIXI, MCP, CI/CD.
- Short readable title.
- Large labels that work on mobile.
- No misspellings.
- No tiny words that should be removed.
- No dense paragraphs.

If text is unclear, simplify labels or regenerate with fewer labels.

## Visual

Check the image or prompt:

- Main idea is clear within 3 seconds.
- One obvious visual hierarchy.
- Warm off-white paper background.
- Hand-drawn black ink linework.
- Soft meaningful colors.
- Arrows are easy to follow.
- Enough blank space.
- Characters are simple and expressive.
- Technical concept is simplified without becoming inaccurate.
- Works as a slide or social media explainer.

Regenerate or revise if the image is cluttered, too corporate, too polished, too decorative, too dark, or too text-heavy.
