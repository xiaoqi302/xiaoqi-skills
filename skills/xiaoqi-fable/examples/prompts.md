## 亡羊补牢

```text
Create a 16:9 horizontal Chinese article illustration in a future fable black-and-white hand-drawn style.

Theme: 亡羊补牢
Core idea: late correction is still valuable if it changes the system, not just repairs the visible hole.
Composition: a quiet sheep pen with one broken fence, a second hidden crack, and a small future gate.

Scene:

Visual style:
clean pure white background, black hand-drawn ink lines, slight uneven linework, subtle woodcut-like hatching, lots of negative space, restrained and quiet.

Annotations:
Add 4 short handwritten Chinese notes: red "损失已发生", red "真正漏洞", blue "系统修补", black "下一次".

Constraints:
No top-left title, no PPT infographic, no cute cartoon, no sci-fi poster, no existing IP character.
```

## 慢就是快

```text
Create a 16:9 horizontal Chinese article illustration in a future fable black-and-white hand-drawn style.

Theme: 慢就是快
Core idea: slow progress wins when it preserves direction, feedback, and endurance.
Composition: a long empty road with many short broken shortcuts crossing it.

Scene:
Xiaobai, a white blank-faced figure in a dark long coat, places a small blue marker on a quiet main road while several abandoned shortcut signs lean in the background. The road is sparse, slightly curved, and has one distant threshold.

Visual style:
near-white background, black hand-drawn ink lines, subtle hatching, lots of negative space, calm and restrained.

Annotations:
Add 4 short handwritten Chinese notes: black "方向", blue "反馈", red "捷径成本", black "耐力".

Constraints:
No large title, no flowchart, no tech UI, no cute cartoon, no commercial illustration.
```
